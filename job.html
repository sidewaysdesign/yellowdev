Manual Operator - Card Personalization (Midnight + Contract)​
Calgary, AB
Design Group Staffing is currently seeking an Equipment Inventory Field Technician for our reputable client based in Calgary. 

Job Type:

This is one-year contract with possible extension that involves a full equipment inventory audit and assessment for Western Canada. This position is based in Calgary but will required extensive travel to the Edson, Grande Prairie and Rainbow Lake areas. As a result, we are open to reviewing candidates local to those areas but would also consider Calgary as a home base assuming they are comfortable with a rotation that requires 80% or more travel. 

Schedule:

Tentatively 10 on, 10 off with either 10 or 12 hours per day. Location Allowance will be provided for travel done outside of the standard home location, for example, if located in Grande Prairie, LOA will be provided for the initial training in Calgary and for work done outside of the Grande Prairie area. If based in Calgary, LOA will only be provided for any time spent outside of Calgary. 

Responsibilities: 

• Complete a full equipment inventory audit for Western Canada 
• Frequent field visits capturing equipment information and location 
• Validate current information within SAP (SEIM) 
• Capture details on any missing equipment 
• Lead the cleansing of master data from Standard development 
• Data mining job books, MDR’s 
• Gather additional information based off inventory needs 
• Other tasks to support the business as required

Qualifications:

• At least 10 years operational and/or technical background within oil and gas operations 
• A background in instrumental electrical/trades/mechanical field preferred 
• Knowledge and understanding of asset classes, characteristics and processes 
• SAP Plant Maintenance proficiency in relation to equipment inventory 
• A valid class 5 Drivers License and a clean driver abstract 
• Safety Tickets: 
o First Aid 
o H2S Alive 
o Confined Spaces 
• Independent and self-motivated with strong investigative and problem-solving skills 
• An excellent communicator with strong interpersonal skills 
• Legible handwriting 
• Proficiency within Microsoft office

Apply:

If you are interested, please apply directly online or submit your resume in Microsoft Word format to: shan@dg.ca

Design Group Staffing would like to thank all candidates for their interest in this opportunity. Due to the volume of resumes we receive we may only be able to respond directly to those candidates being selected for an interview.
We encourage you to visit www.dg.ca regularly as new and exciting employment opportunities become available daily!
